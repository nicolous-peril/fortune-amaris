About Us

Fortune Amaris Diagnostic Center is a duly-licensed institution-based facility with Department Of Health (DOH) Accreditation No. 13-0578-19-CL-2. We offer medical services for APEs and pre-employment requirements. Our services includes, but not limited to, complete laboratory, X-ray, ultrasound, ECG, 2D echo, and drug-testing. We also offer mobile clinic services to increase accessibility of our offerings making it easier for individuals to receive essential medical services.
